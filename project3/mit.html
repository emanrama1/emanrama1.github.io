Just University


Jordan University of Science and Technology stands out as a jewel among Jordanian universities,
as described by the late great King Hussein, may he rest in peace. Like other national institutions,
it holds a special place in the eyes and heart of His Majesty King Abdullah II, the Hashemite king
of Jordan and the son of the late great King Hussein.It serves as an iconic symbol for education and
scientific research in our beloved country,boasting an exceptional faculty and administrative staff.
The institution attracts knowledge seekers from our nation and a significant number of students from
neighboring and friendly countries.Today, our university has evolved into a prestigious and eminent
scientific academic establishment,one that we take great pride in being affiliated with.
